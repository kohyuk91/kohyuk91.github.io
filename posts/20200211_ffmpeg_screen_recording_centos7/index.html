FFmpeg Screen Recording - CentOS7

Tue, Feb 11, 2020

No Audio

$ ffmpeg -framerate 30 -s 1920x1080 -f x11grab -i :0.0 -c:v libx264 -pix_fmt yuv420p ~/desktop_$(date +%Y%m%dT%H%M%S).mp4
